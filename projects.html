Nutribin Ecosystem
Dec 2025 - Apr 2026
Associated with University of Caloocan City

Creating a smart compost system that accepts only soft or small biodegradable waste, automatically mixing and drying it to accelerate safe decomposition. It continuously monitors fertilizer quality by tracking NPK, pH, temperature, moisture, and key gases. The system controls environmental conditions and logs nutrient and safety metrics to ensure the final fertilizer is safe for plants. The system includes a web and mobile application for real-time monitoring, data visualization, and control, integrated with a hardware setup using Arduino Uno Q and ESP32 for sensor data acquisition, automation, and system management.
